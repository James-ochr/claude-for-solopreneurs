## If Something Goes Wrong
- **CLAUDE.md or dream missing:** Ask "what's the one thing you'd want Claude to build for your business right now?" and build from that.
- **Page doesn't open:** Tell them the file is saved in the current folder and they can double-click it.
- **They want to keep iterating:** Let them. Each tweak reinforces the pattern. After a few, move to the finale.
- **Form link doesn't load:** Give them the link as text and tell them they can open it anytime.
